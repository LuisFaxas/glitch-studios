    ```tsx
    <option key={service.slug} value={service.name}>
    ```

    ```tsx
    <option key={service.slug} value={service.slug}>
    ```

    This is a one-line fix. The service-tabs.tsx passes `?service=${service.slug}` in the URL. Changing to `value={service.slug}` makes the option values match the URL param.
