## What was built

`isTechPathActive(itemHref, pathname)` and `stripTechPrefix(path)` helpers in
`src/lib/tech/nav.ts`, wired into the three pathname-comparison callsites:

- `src/components/layout/bottom-tab-bar.tsx` (mobile bottom tab bar)
- `src/components/layout/tile-nav.tsx` collapsed branch
- `src/components/layout/tile-nav.tsx` expanded branch

`Tile` gains a `data-active` passthrough so Playwright can use a stable
non-Tailwind selector. The bottom-tab `<nav>` carries `data-tab-bar=""` for
the same reason.
