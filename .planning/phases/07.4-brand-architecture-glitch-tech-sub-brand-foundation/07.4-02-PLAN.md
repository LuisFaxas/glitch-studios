Research recommendation: use the same controller — it reads pathname internally and just needs to recognize `/tech` as "homepage." Option: pass a `homepagePaths` prop to make it generic, OR just have the Tech layout wrap its own `<SidebarProvider defaultCollapsed={isTechHome} isHomepage={isTechHome} />` inline. For minimal surface area this plan takes Option B (inline SidebarProvider) to avoid touching the shared controller.
