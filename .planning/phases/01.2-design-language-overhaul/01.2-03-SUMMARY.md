## Decisions Made
- **LayoutGrid as center menu trigger:** Replaced the old auth-focused bottom tab (sign in/out) with a grid icon that opens the full navigation overlay. Auth moved into the overlay.
- **Full widget replication in overlay:** MobileNavOverlay includes NowPlaying, StudioStatus, and Social widgets for parity with desktop sidebar.
- **ServiceGrid over ServiceTabs:** Master-detail split provides better information density on desktop; accordion on mobile keeps content accessible without horizontal scrolling.
- **100ms glitch duration on cards:** Subtle enough for frequent browsing, distinct from the 200ms used on navigation tiles.
- **Explicit hex values everywhere:** All Tailwind gray-* utility classes replaced with explicit hex values (#111111, #222222, #888888, etc.) for strict monochrome enforcement.
